5 Essential Car Care Tips for Extreme Summer in Qatar

April 12, 2025
By Michael K.

Driving in Qatar's scorching summer heat presents unique challenges for your vehicle. At Safeway, we've seen how extreme temperatures — often exceeding 45°C — can affect engine performance, tyre health, and AC efficiency. To keep your ride smooth and breakdown-free, follow these essential car care insights from our workshop experts.

1. Keep Your Cooling System in Top Shape

Your engine's cooling system works twice as hard in summer. Regularly check coolant levels, inspect hoses for leaks or brittleness, and flush the radiator as recommended. Overheating is one of the leading causes of roadside emergencies in Doha. A properly maintained cooling system prevents catastrophic engine damage and ensures peak performance even during afternoon heatwaves.

"Nearly 40% of vehicle breakdowns in Qatar during July–August are related to cooling system failures. A simple coolant top-up and radiator check can save thousands in repair costs."

2. Tyre Pressure & Tread Inspection

Hot asphalt increases tyre pressure and accelerates wear. Under-inflated or worn tyres significantly raise the risk of blowouts. We recommend checking tyre pressure monthly (including the spare) and ensuring the tread depth meets safety standards. Consider nitrogen inflation for more stable pressure in high heat. Safeway offers complimentary tyre checks with every service appointment.

3. Air Conditioning Performance

Nothing makes a summer drive unbearable like a failing AC. Ensure your cabin air filter is clean, refrigerant level is optimal, and the compressor runs quietly. A weak airflow or strange odours indicate blocked vents or bacteria growth. Our AC rejuvenation service restores cooling power and eliminates musty smells, making every journey comfortable for your family.

4. Battery Health Under High Temperatures

Extreme heat can evaporate battery fluid and damage internal structures. Many drivers believe cold weather is harder on batteries, but summer heat is equally destructive. Have your battery load-tested, clean any corrosion from terminals, and check for swelling. Safeway’s mobile unit can test your battery on the spot, giving peace of mind before long drives.

5. Fluids and Belts Inspection

From engine oil to brake fluid and transmission fluid — high temperatures degrade lubricants faster. Follow manufacturer intervals for oil changes and use high-viscosity oil suitable for Gulf climate. Also inspect serpentine belts for cracks or glazing. A snapped belt could cost you steering assist and cooling fan operation. Scheduling a comprehensive summer checkup ensures no hidden weak points.

Our goal is to keep your car running efficiently regardless of the outside temperature. Visit our workshop in Industrial Area or book a mobile service — your safety is our priority.
